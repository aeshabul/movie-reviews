The Batman (2022)

Genre: Action, Crime, Drama

Runtime: 2h 56m

Rating: ⭐ 4.3 / 5

Story Overview

Batman faces Gotham’s most dangerous criminal, The Riddler, uncovering corruption that runs deep within the city.

Review

The Batman is dark, slow-burn and deeply character-driven. Robert Pattinson delivers a raw and intense performance, offering a grounded and realistic take on the iconic superhero.

Cast

Robert Pattinson, Zoë Kravitz, Paul Dano

Final Verdict

A bold and grounded Batman reboot that rewards patient viewers.
