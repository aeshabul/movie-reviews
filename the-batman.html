The Batman (2022)

⭐ 4.3 / 5 Action Crime Drama 2h 56m

Matt Reeves delivers a dark, grounded, and detective-driven take on Batman, focusing on atmosphere, character psychology, and Gotham’s deep-rooted corruption.

Watch Trailer ← Back to Home

Critic Review

The Batman is a slow-burn noir thriller that prioritizes investigation over spectacle. Robert Pattinson’s portrayal is restrained and intense, presenting a vulnerable and obsessive Dark Knight.

Cast

Robert Pattinson, Zoë Kravitz, Paul Dano

Final Verdict

A bold and atmospheric reboot that redefines Batman as a detective first, superhero second.
