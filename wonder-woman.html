Story & Review

Wonder Woman follows Diana, an Amazonian princess who leaves her hidden island home after an American pilot crashes nearby and tells her about a massive war raging across the world.

Gal Gadot shines as Wonder Woman, bringing strength, compassion and grace to the role. The film balances action, emotion and humor while delivering a strong message about humanity.

The first half of the movie is especially strong, though the climax feels slightly weaker compared to the buildup.
